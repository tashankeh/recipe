Recipe

Chocolate Cake

This chocolate recipe is classic. Enjoy!

Difficulty Preparation Cooking Total Time
Easy 10 min 25 min 35 min

Ingredients

Dark Chocolate: 200g

Butter: 200g

Flour: 80g

Eggs: 4

Instructions

1

 Melt the chocolate and the butter together.

2

 Mix the sugar, egg and the flour.

3

 Mix everythng to get a smooth dough.

4

 Bake in the oven.
